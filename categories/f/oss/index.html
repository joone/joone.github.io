OSCON 2014
I attended OSCON 2014 held in Portland, Oregon. I think Portland is the Mecca of Open Source because OSCON is held there every year and Intel Open Source Technology center is also in Hillsboro near Portland. In addition, Linus Torvalds is based in Portland. I wanted to attend some sessions this year, but I didn’t see many topics related to my job. There are more high-level topics above system level.
